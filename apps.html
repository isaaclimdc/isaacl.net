iOS Apps

Here are some apps that you might find useful.
And also look pretty on your screen.

CV Email @isaaclimdc Blog

HisWords (iPhone)

A free gesture-based app designed with the sole purpose of getting you to passage in the Bible as quickly as possible, with as few taps as possible. HisWords sports a minimalist design and a clean interface built from the ground up, with every element of its interface customized solely for this app.

The app is crafted for lightning-fast reference to the Bible, either during Sunday service, or any time during the week when you're prompted with an inspirational verse.

AndrewQPA (iPhone)

This is a simple app for calculating your Carnegie Mellon University Quality Point Average (QPA). It allows you to enter your courses and their corresponding grades for individual semesters, and keeps a record of cumulative and non-cumulative data for you.

AndrewQPA is designed purely for Carnegie Mellon students. Its UI is completely custom-made with Tartan pride, and looks beautiful on the iPhone 5 and the Retina Display!

BusTransfersSG (iPhone/iPad)

A very simple utility to help remind you the amount of time you have left to transfer to another SMRT/SBS bus in Singapore. It is currently just a very simple timer to make sure your next trip is not counted as a new trip, which would waste you money in the long run.

The app sends you a single push notification when you have 10 minutes left to get on your next bus. It tries to do all this in the most non-intrusive way possible. There's also a visual aid in the form of a bus making its way across the screen to let you know how much time you have left!

THB to SGD Converter (iPhone)

Easy and lightweight converter from Thai Baht to Singapore Dollar. It fetches the latest hourly rate from OpenExchangeRates, and automatically does conversions for you. If no internet is present (like most times during your holiday in Bangkok!), the app will use the latest fetched rate. No worries there!

Social Poster (iPhone/iPad)

Social Poster is a lightweight, handy tool that allows you to post statuses to both your Facebook wall and your Twitter profile page simultaneously. Now you can allow all your friends on both social networks to see what's going on in your life with just one click!

With the new redesign and recode, you can do all this in style natively on both your iPhone and your iPad!

Shorten.er (iPhone)

Shorten.er is a free, handy and simple app that follows through 3 foolproof steps to convert a long, unmanageable URL into a short one using the j.mp service. After shortening your desired URL, you can copy it to clipboard to paste in other apps, or share it in a tweet or email.

Do this all using a cute and friendly interface, designed to make you feel comfortable and at home!

Leave (iPhone)

A simple app that helps you record your off/leave days at your workplace. It presents a very simple interface to you, allowing you to either award or deduct leave days, giving a date and a reason for each entry.

Easily keep track of your half-days as well: something that many leave apps out there can't help you with. It's a simple concept, yet something vital: not all of add halves that quickly! The app also sorts your entries by date, so you can easily scroll through and pick out where that 1.5 days went!

TaskHelper (iPhone/iPad)

TaskHelper is a simple tool that helps you manage your ongoing tasks. With a no-frills yet beautiful interface, TaskHelper dutifully remembers your important to-dos and presents them to you in a pleasing and direct way. Bring TaskHelper along with you on either your iPhone, iPod touch, or even iPad, and manage your tasks on-the-go! Never forget another important task!

Radial (iPad)

Radial is an innovative custom keyboard app for the iPad. Built specifically for use with only your two thumbs, Radial allows you to type on your iPad in a whole new way. If you’re the kind of user who holds his/her iPad upright with both palms in the horizontal orientation, you’ll definitely recognize how difficult it is to reach the center alphabets on the standard keyboard. Radial was created to help you with that.
